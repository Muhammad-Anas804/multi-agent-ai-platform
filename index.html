❓ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is VenturePilot?▸
VenturePilot is an AI-powered business intelligence platform that analyzes your business ideas using 5 specialized agents: Research, Marketing, Finance, Risk, and CEO Decision.
How long does an analysis take?▸
A full analysis typically takes 30-90 seconds depending on your internet connection and the complexity of your idea.
Is my data secure?▸
Yes! Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell or share your business ideas with third parties. Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2-SHA256.
What is the Free plan limit?▸
The Free plan allows 3 analyses per month. Upgrade to Starter ($9/mo) for 50 analyses or Pro ($29/mo) for unlimited analyses.
Can I download my reports?▸
Yes! After running an analysis, click the "Download" button to save your full report as a .txt file. You can also email it directly from the platform.
How do I cancel my subscription?▸
You can cancel anytime from your Profile settings. Contact us at muhammadanas.ragdev@gmail.com for immediate assistance.
What AI models power VenturePilot?▸
VenturePilot uses Groq LLM API with advanced language models for fast, accurate business analysis. Our agents use LangGraph for multi-step reasoning.
